Further helplines are available below,
some of which specialise in specific areas.

You may find it easier to talk with someone with a deep understanding of your specific circumstances.

It can also be helpful to have a few options for who to call, in case you find that there is a long waiting time for one of the services. The majority of these helplines provide advice and support to loved ones too.
